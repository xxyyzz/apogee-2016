Paper Abstract Submission

INSTRUCTIONS :
  • The results for the abstracts will be declared by 15th November 2015.
  • Participants have to upload their abstracts for screening in the appropriate format on the Apogee 2016 portal.The portal will be accessible from 1st October 2015. The prescribed format must be followed, failing which may lead to disqualification of the participants. The deadline for abstract submission is 19th October 2015, 11:59 PM.
  • The abstract should be submitted only in the format provided below.
  • Only genuine and original paper abstracts should be uploaded. Abstracts found copied from any source like internet/ journals etc. will be disqualified.
  • Only undergraduate students of any recognized university can participate.
  • A maximum of 2 students per team are allowed to present a paper (one as author and another as co-author). Participants from different institutions can also form a team.
  • The abstract will be judged on the basis of their innovation and knowledge of the field.
  • Plagiarized content will be strictly dealt with and would lead to participant's disqualification. In case of any discrepancy, organizers decision will be final and binding.
  • Participants can present one paper in one category only. Also, participants can present more than one paper. However, the abstracts should be submitted separately.
