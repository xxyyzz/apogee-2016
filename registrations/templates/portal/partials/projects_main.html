Project Abstract Submission

The first round of pitching for Bitsian projects will take place from 3-11 Feb 2016.

Following are the guidelines for Project Pitching:

  • People who do not have a prototype will NOT be allowed to present their project.
  • The time limit for each presentation is 5 minutes.
  • Reach the venue on time and book slots for the presentation.
  • Only genuine reasons for not being able to present will be accepted.
  • Every team member is expected to be there for the presentation.
  • Please keep checking the portal for any further updates.
  • Projects that require internet connectivity, should mention it in advance so arrangements can be made.
