The first round of pitching for Bitsian projects will take place from 3-11 Feb 2016.

Following are the guidelines for Project Pitching:

  • 1.   People who do not have a prototype will NOT be allowed to present their project.
  • 2.   The time limit for each presentation is 5 minutes.
  • 3.   Reach the venue on time and book slots for the presentation.
  • 4.   Only genuine reasons for not being able to present will be accepted.
  • 5.   Every team member is expected to be there for the presentation.
  • 6.   Please keep checking the portal for any further updates.
  • 7.   Projects that require internet connectivity, should mention it in advance so arrangements can be made.

The deadline for submission of Project Abstract was 28th October, 2015 11:59pm. Project abstracts are no longer accepted.
