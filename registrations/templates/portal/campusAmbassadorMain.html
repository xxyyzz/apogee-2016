What is a Campus Ambassador ?

A Campus Ambassador is responsible for publicity for APOGEE, the annual technical extravaganza of BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus, and is responsible for introducing and encouraging student participation in the fest, in his/her area or college.

To APOGEE he/she is the chief student representative of the college and is the coordinator for any relevant activity conducted in his/her college for publicity through social media, College notice boards, Root-mails etc.

Why be a Campus Ambassador ?

Campus Ambassador Program, this time is powered by YOUTH4WORK, an established talent assessment company. A Campus Ambassador will get an official certificate through BITS-Pilani for pursuing a Marketing Internship Programme for 3 months recognized by YOUTH4WORK.

Moreover, he/she will be entitled to awards and special incentives like exemptions from registration fees, exclusive merchandise, recommendations etc. based on their performance in the programme.

Being an integral part of BITS-Pilani’s technical fest is itself a thing worth considering.

Things to know about the programme
  • The programme will last for about a duration of 3 months (till the end of APOGEE-2016)
  • The ambassadors will be in a continuous communication link with the organizers of the fest.
  • The ambassadors with exceptional performance will be rewarded with awards and recommendations.
  • Only active ambassadors who meet the required target will be considered for any kind of reward.
  • The organizers will extend an active support in helping the campus ambassadors accomplish their tasks as well as they can.
